Single Pagers

This is just a little collection of single page HTML/CSS/JavaScript applications. I wanted to challenge myself to write small, self-contained, to-the-point apps that don't rely on any server-side code. Updated occasionally. Enjoy!

Comparer

A simple app for finding the right deal - enter the prices and quantities of each item, and the best per-unit price is highlighted!

Ugly Clock

A terrible-looking clock written as a joke.

Horadric Cube

A very stripped-down implementation of the Horadric Cube from Diablo 2.

Meme Generator

A simple meme generator. Even allows you to download when you're done!

Dragon

A silly little app using canvas and sprite sheets that lets you control a flying dragon.

Conway's Game of Life

A canvas-based implementation of everyone's favorite zero-player game.
